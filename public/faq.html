Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Find a book you'd like to swap
  2. See what users have it
  3. Find a book that you can afford given your points
  4. Specify a user and send a request to that user for the book
  5. The book owner is notified of the request
  6. The book owner can accept or reject the swap
  7. If the swap is accepted, the asked amount of points is transferred to from your account the book owner's
  8. The former book owner ships the book
  9. You get the receive the book and enjoy!
Users can earn points by accepting swap requests for their books from other users
If it has been more than 5 days from the anticipated arrival, contact bookcops@books4books.com
If you don't see an answer to your question, email your question to help@books4books.com and one of our Booksperts will be happy to help
